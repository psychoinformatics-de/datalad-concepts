Three classes cover processes and activities:

- organizational: `Project`
- investigative: `Study`
- generative: `Genesis`

A number of "classifier-type" classes. These do not support PROV annotation,
but are themselves useful for PROV annotations of another types.

- `XYZDataType`
- `XYZConvention`
- `XYZDistributionPartType`
- `XYZConcept`
- `XYZBibliographicType`
- `XYZInstrumentType`
- `XYZPublicationVenue`
- `XYZSubjectType`
- `XYZTopic`
- `XYZRule`
- `XYZAgentRole`
